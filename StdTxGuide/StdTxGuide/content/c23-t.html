1. Granuloma Inguinale

Treatment

Several antimicrobial regimens have been effective, but only a limited number of controlled trials have been published376. Treatment has been shown to halt progression of lesions, and healing typically proceeds inward from the ulcer margins; prolonged therapy is usually required to permit granulation and reepithelialization of the ulcers.
