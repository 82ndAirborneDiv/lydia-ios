1. Syphilis
  2. Primary and Secondary
Recommended Regimen for Adults*
Benzathine penicillin G 2.4 million units IM in a single dose

* Recommendations for treating syphilis in persons with HIV infection and pregnant women are discussed later in this report (see Syphilis among Persons with HIV infection and Syphilis in Pregnancy).


Recommended Regimen for Infants and Children
Benzathine penicillin G 50,000 units/kg IM, up to the adult dose of 2.4 million units in a single dose
