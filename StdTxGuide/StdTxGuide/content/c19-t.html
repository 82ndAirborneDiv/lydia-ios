  3. Conjunctivitis

In the only published study of the treatment of gonococcal conjunctivitis among adults, all 12 study participants responded to a single 1-g IM injection of ceftriaxone 571. Because gonococcal conjunctivitis is uncommon, and data on treatment of gonococcal conjunctivitis in adults is limited, consultation with an infectious disease specialist should be considered.
