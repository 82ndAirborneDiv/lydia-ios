Recommended Regimen
Primary and Secondary Syphilis among Persons with HIV Infection
Benzathine penicillin G, 2.4 million units IM in a single dose

Recommended Regimen for Early Latent Syphilis among Persons with HIV infection
Benzathine penicillin G, 2.4 million units IM in a single dose

Recommended Regimen for Late Latent Syphilis among Persons with HIV infection
Benzathine penicillin G, at weekly doses of 2.4 million units for 3 weeks
