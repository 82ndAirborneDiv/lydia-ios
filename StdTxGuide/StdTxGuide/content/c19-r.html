Gonorrhea -> Adolescents & Adults -> Disseminated Gonococcal Infection


Recommended Regimen - Disseminated Gonococcal Infection (DGI)
Ceftriaxone 1 g IM or IV every 24 hours
Alternative Regimen - Disseminated Gonococcal Infection (DGI)
Cefotaxime 1 g IV every 8 hours
OR
Ceftizoxime 1 g IV every 8 hours
